# QBR Prep Guide

Owner: Customer Success
Last reviewed: 2026-02-23

## Purpose

Quarterly business reviews help customers understand value, adoption, risks, and next steps.

## Prep Inputs

- usage trends;
- support case themes;
- open product feedback;
- renewal date;
- stakeholder changes;
- success criteria;
- unresolved blockers;
- expansion opportunities.

## Review Requirements

Customer Success should confirm metrics with Data Analytics when numbers will be presented externally. Product review is needed for roadmap discussion. Legal review is needed for unusual commitments.

## Follow-Up

Send a summary with decisions, owners, dates, and open questions within two business days.
