# Customer Onboarding Playbook

Owner: Customer Success
Last reviewed: 2026-03-13

## Purpose

This playbook guides the first 60 days after a customer signs.

## Kickoff

The Customer Success Manager confirms goals, stakeholders, timeline, success criteria, integrations, security constraints, and communication cadence.

## Implementation

Implementation owner coordinates configuration, training, data import if applicable, and launch readiness. Product or Engineering joins when the customer needs non-standard technical guidance.

## Handoff From Sales

Sales should provide contract context, promised outcomes, key stakeholders, decision criteria, implementation risks, and any commitments made during procurement.

## Success Review

Within 60 days, the Customer Success Manager should confirm adoption, unresolved blockers, support trends, and next milestone.
