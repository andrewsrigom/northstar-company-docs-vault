# Implementation Handoff

Owner: Customer Success
Last reviewed: 2026-02-17

## Purpose

This handoff ensures Sales context becomes implementation context after contract signature.

## Required Fields

- customer goals;
- contract start date;
- implementation deadline;
- stakeholders;
- integrations;
- security requirements;
- training needs;
- promised outcomes;
- risks;
- open commercial questions.

## Handoff Meeting

Sales, Customer Success, and Implementation meet within five business days of signature for strategic customers. Smaller customers may use async handoff notes.

## Gaps

If a promised outcome is unclear or not supported by current product capability, Customer Success escalates to Sales leadership and Product before confirming the implementation plan.
