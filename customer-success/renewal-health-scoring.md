# Renewal Health Scoring

Owner: Customer Success
Last reviewed: 2026-03-21

## Purpose

Health scoring helps Customer Success identify renewal risk early.

## Signals

Positive signals include active usage, executive engagement, achieved success criteria, low unresolved support burden, and clear expansion interest.

Risk signals include declining usage, unresolved incidents, stakeholder turnover, repeated support escalations, unpaid invoices, missing executive sponsor, or competitor evaluation.

## Score Review

Customer Success reviews strategic account health monthly. Renewal risks within 120 days should be shared with Sales, Support, Product, and Finance when relevant.

## Escalation

Escalate high-risk renewals when customer impact requires Product prioritization, executive engagement, contract review, or billing resolution.
