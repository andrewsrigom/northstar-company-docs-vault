# Customer Escalation Communications

Owner: Customer Success and Support
Last reviewed: 2026-03-15

## Purpose

This guide helps teams communicate during customer escalations without creating conflicting commitments.

## Communication Owner

Customer Success owns relationship communication for named accounts. Support owns case updates. Engineering provides technical status through the internal incident or escalation channel.

## Message Requirements

Customer updates should include current status, known impact, next update time, workaround if available, and owner. Avoid root cause claims until Engineering confirms them.

## Legal Or Security Topics

Legal review is required before discussing breach notification, contractual remedies, credits, or formal commitments. IT Security leads security incident language.
