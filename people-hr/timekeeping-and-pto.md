# Timekeeping and PTO

## Purpose

This policy explains how Northstar Company employees record work time, request paid time off, and handle schedule changes.

## Recording Hours

Hourly employees must record time in WorkTrack by the end of each workday. Salaried employees do not submit daily timecards unless their manager requests project-level tracking for a specific initiative.

If an hourly employee forgets to submit time, they should notify their manager in Slack and submit a correction in WorkTrack within two business days. Managers approve corrections every Friday before payroll closes.

## PTO Requests

Employees request PTO in WorkTrack at least five business days before the planned absence. Managers should approve or decline PTO within two business days.

For same-day sick leave, employees should notify their manager before the start of their shift when possible and enter the sick leave request in WorkTrack when they return.

## Coverage Planning

Teams should keep a shared coverage note for planned absences longer than three business days. The note should include:

- dates out;
- primary coverage owner;
- customer or internal commitments that need attention;
- unresolved blockers.

## Escalation

If a PTO request is urgent and the manager is unavailable, the employee should contact the People Operations inbox. People Operations can approve emergency leave but does not override business coverage without manager follow-up.
