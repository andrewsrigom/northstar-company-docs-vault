# Learning and Development

Owner: People Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-02-20

## Purpose

Northstar supports role-related learning that improves employee effectiveness, customer outcomes, or internal operations.

## Annual Learning Budget

Full-time employees may request up to 1,200 USD per calendar year for approved courses, books, conferences, certifications, or workshops. Managers approve business relevance. Finance confirms budget availability and reimbursement rules.

## Request Process

Employees should submit a Learning Request at least ten business days before payment is due. The request should include provider, cost, learning objective, time away from work if any, and how the learning applies to the role.

## Conferences

Conference travel requires both learning approval and travel approval. Employees presenting externally also need Marketing or Legal review when the presentation mentions customers, product roadmap, metrics, or company strategy.

## Sharing Back

Employees who use more than 500 USD of the annual budget should share notes, a short summary, or a team learning session within 30 days of completion.
