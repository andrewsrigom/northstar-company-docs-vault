# Benefits Overview

## Health Benefits

Northstar Company offers medical, dental, and vision plans to full-time employees working at least 30 hours per week. Coverage begins on the first day of the month after the employee's start date.

Employees can change benefit elections during annual enrollment or after a qualifying life event such as marriage, birth, adoption, or loss of other coverage.

## Wellness Stipend

Full-time employees receive a quarterly wellness stipend of 150 USD. Eligible uses include gym memberships, fitness classes, mental health apps, ergonomic equipment, and preventive health programs.

Employees submit wellness stipend receipts through SpendFlow using the category `Wellness`. Receipts must be submitted within 60 days.

## Learning Budget

Each employee receives an annual learning budget of 1,000 USD for role-related training, books, certifications, and conferences. Manager approval is required before spending more than 250 USD on a single item.

## Questions

Employees should contact People Operations for plan eligibility, enrollment changes, leave questions, or benefit documents.
