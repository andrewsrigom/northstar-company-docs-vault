# Compensation Cycle FAQ

Owner: People Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-01-15

## What Is Reviewed?

Northstar reviews base compensation, role level, market data, performance evidence, internal equity, and business budget during the annual compensation cycle.

## Who Makes Decisions?

Managers submit recommendations. Department leaders review consistency. People Operations checks process integrity and pay equity. Finance confirms budget. Executive approval is required for material budget changes.

## Are Promotions Included?

Promotion recommendations may be reviewed during the compensation cycle, but promotion approval depends on scope, role expectations, business need, and sustained performance evidence.

## Out-of-Cycle Changes

Out-of-cycle compensation changes are limited to role changes, retention risks, market corrections, or documented process errors. Managers should not promise changes before People Operations and Finance review.

## Employee Questions

Employees should discuss compensation questions with their manager first. If the concern involves process fairness, pay equity, or policy interpretation, People Operations should be included.
