# Remote Work Guidelines

Owner: People Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-02-12

## Purpose

Northstar is remote-first with optional office hubs. These guidelines explain default expectations for remote work, time zones, and team availability.

## Core Hours

Teams should define at least four overlapping collaboration hours on normal business days. Managers may adjust core hours based on customer coverage, support shifts, or regional needs.

Employees should keep their calendar current, use Slack status for same-day availability changes, and document handoffs before being offline for more than one business day.

## Work Location Changes

Short-term domestic work location changes under 30 calendar days require manager notice. International work location changes require People Operations review before travel because payroll, tax, benefits, immigration, and security requirements may apply.

## Office Hubs

Employees may use office hubs for focused work, team meetings, or customer sessions. Visitors must follow the visitor and office access process owned by Operations.

## Exceptions

Remote work exceptions involving payroll jurisdiction, equipment shipping, security controls, or medical accommodation should be routed to People Operations. Managers should not approve long-term location exceptions without People review.
