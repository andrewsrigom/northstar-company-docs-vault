# Employee Offboarding Checklist

Owner: People Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-03-22

## Purpose

This checklist coordinates People Operations, IT, Finance, managers, and Operations when an employee leaves Northstar.

## Manager Responsibilities

- Confirm final working date with People Operations.
- Create a transition plan for active projects, customers, vendors, and approvals.
- Transfer ownership for recurring meetings, dashboards, documents, and systems.
- Confirm whether equipment return is needed.

## IT Responsibilities

IT disables access according to the termination type and timing confirmed by People Operations. For involuntary departures, IT coordinates with People Operations before any access changes are visible to the employee.

## Finance Responsibilities

Finance reviews outstanding reimbursements, purchase cards, vendor approvals, and payroll timing. Managers should confirm whether any expenses are expected after the final working date.

## Documentation Cleanup

The manager should identify shared documents owned by the departing employee and assign new owners before the final working day. Critical runbooks should not remain in a personal drive.
