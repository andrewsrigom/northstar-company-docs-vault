# Business Continuity Overview

Owner: Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-02-21

## Purpose

This overview explains how Northstar handles office closures, system outages, vendor disruptions, and events that affect normal operations.

## Continuity Priorities

1. employee safety;
2. customer commitments;
3. security and legal obligations;
4. payroll and finance operations;
5. internal productivity.

## Team Plans

Each department should keep a lightweight continuity note covering critical systems, backup owners, manual workarounds, vendor dependencies, and communication channels.

## Activation

Operations may open a continuity coordination channel when an event affects multiple teams or lasts longer than one business day. Security incidents remain owned by IT Security.

## After Action

For material events, Operations creates a summary within five business days covering impact, timeline, decisions, customer communication, and follow-up actions.
