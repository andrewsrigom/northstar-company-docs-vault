# Office Supply And Facilities Requests

Owner: Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-01-29

## Purpose

Employees use this process for office supplies, repairs, room setup, mail handling, and workspace issues.

## Request Types

- office supplies;
- meeting room setup;
- visitor support;
- building maintenance;
- mail and package questions;
- ergonomic equipment for office desks;
- event setup.

## Submission

Open an Operations request with location, date needed, business purpose, quantity, budget if known, and photos when relevant.

## Approval

Routine supplies do not require manager approval. Purchases above 500 USD, branded materials, furniture, or recurring services may require Finance or department owner approval.
