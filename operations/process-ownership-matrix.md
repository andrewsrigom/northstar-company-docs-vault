# Process Ownership Matrix

Owner: Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-04-02

## Purpose

This matrix gives employees a starting point when they do not know who owns a cross-functional process.

## Ownership

- employee onboarding: People Operations, with IT for access and devices;
- vendor onboarding: Operations, with Legal, Finance, and IT Security reviews;
- invoice intake: Finance, with requester confirmation;
- office access: Operations, with IT Security for badge systems;
- customer escalation routing: Support, Customer Success, and department owner;
- product launch readiness: Product, with Engineering, Marketing, Support, Legal, and Sales;
- security incident response: IT Security, with Legal and affected department owners.

## Approver vs Owner

The owner coordinates the process. Approvers make specific risk decisions. A process may move forward only when required approvers have completed their part.

## Maintenance

Operations updates this page monthly based on repeated support cases and documentation gaps.
