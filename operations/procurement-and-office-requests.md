# Procurement and Office Requests

## Procurement Thresholds

Employees can request purchases through SpendFlow. Purchases under 500 USD require manager approval. Purchases from 500 USD to 5,000 USD require department director approval. Purchases above 5,000 USD require Finance review before commitment.

## Required Information

Every purchase request should include:

- vendor name;
- business purpose;
- expected cost;
- department budget code;
- renewal or subscription terms;
- security or legal review status when applicable.

## Office Requests

Facilities requests such as badges, seating, repairs, and shipping supplies should be submitted through OfficeDesk. Urgent safety issues should be reported by phone to the Facilities on-call number.

## Handoffs

If a request includes a new software vendor, Operations routes the request to IT and Legal before Finance approval. Employees should not sign vendor terms directly.
