# Vendor Renewal Calendar

Owner: Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-03-26

## Purpose

Operations tracks vendor renewals so business owners have enough time to decide whether to renew, renegotiate, or cancel.

## Renewal Timeline

- 90 days before renewal: Operations alerts business owner and renewal owner.
- 60 days before renewal: business owner confirms need, usage, budget, and alternatives.
- 45 days before renewal: Legal reviews contract changes if needed.
- 30 days before renewal: Finance confirms budget and payment path.
- 15 days before renewal: requester confirms final decision.

## Required Fields

Vendor record should include renewal date, notice period, business owner, system owner, renewal owner, annual spend, contract link, data classification, and cancellation instructions.

## Escalation

If renewal owner is missing, Operations escalates to the department lead. Auto-renewals above 5,000 USD should not proceed without business owner confirmation.
