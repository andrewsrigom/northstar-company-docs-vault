# Office Visitor Access

Owner: Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-02-11

## Purpose

This procedure covers visitor registration, office access, and guest expectations for Northstar office hubs.

## Visitor Requirements

Employees hosting visitors must submit a visitor request at least one business day before arrival. The request should include visitor name, company, host, visit purpose, date, arrival time, and whether a meeting room is needed.

## Check-In

Visitors must sign in, wear a visitor badge, and remain with their host unless Operations approves a specific exception. Visitors should not enter secure work areas without an employee escort.

## Customer Visits

Customer visits should be coordinated with Sales or Customer Success. Marketing review may be needed for photography, recording, or public posts about the visit.

## Security Issues

Lost badges, suspicious behavior, or unauthorized access should be reported to Operations and IT Security immediately.
