# Vendor Onboarding Handoff

## Purpose

This procedure explains how Operations coordinates a new vendor from request intake through Legal, Finance, IT Security, and the requesting team.

## Intake Requirements

The requester should submit a Vendor Intake ticket with:

- business purpose;
- expected annual spend;
- vendor contact;
- data types the vendor will access;
- requested start date;
- department owner;
- whether a contract or order form is required.

Operations rejects incomplete requests and asks the requester to update the ticket before routing.

## Routing Rules

Legal review is required when there is a contract, customer or employee data, regulated data, auto-renewal longer than one year, or annual spend above 5,000 USD.

Finance review is required for new vendors, payment terms, budget changes, and spend above the manager's approval limit.

IT Security review is required when the vendor needs system access, SSO, API credentials, customer data, employee data, or security questionnaire approval.

## Handoff Checklist

Operations should confirm:

1. Legal approval or documented no-contract exception;
2. Finance vendor setup and budget owner approval;
3. IT Security approval for access or data handling;
4. requester acceptance of implementation owner and renewal owner;
5. storage of final contract or purchase record in the vendor folder.

## Escalation

If a vendor is urgent but review is incomplete, Operations may request an exception review from the department leader. Legal, Finance, and IT Security can block launch when their risk area is unresolved.
