# Product Feedback Handoff

Owner: Support and Product
Last reviewed: 2026-03-03

## Purpose

This process explains how Support turns customer or employee feedback into product signals without promising roadmap outcomes.

## When To Handoff

Create a product feedback handoff when the case includes a repeated request, workflow blocker, usability confusion, missing integration, reporting gap, or clear customer impact.

## Required Fields

- requester type;
- account or team impacted;
- workflow context;
- current workaround;
- frequency if known;
- business impact;
- screenshots or examples;
- related tickets.

## Product Review

Product reviews feedback weekly and may link it to roadmap themes, discovery notes, or existing feature requests. Support should not promise priority or timing unless Product has approved the message.
