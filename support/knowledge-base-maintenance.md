# Knowledge Base Maintenance

Owner: Support
Last reviewed: 2026-02-27

## Purpose

Support maintains reviewed answers for repeated employee questions and links them to source policies.

## Reviewed Answer Criteria

A reviewed answer should have a source policy, policy owner approval, plain-language answer, next action, escalation path, and review date.

## Monthly Cleanup

Support reviews top search misses, cases marked as documentation gaps, stale reviewed answers, and questions with conflicting citations.

## Retiring Answers

Retire a reviewed answer when the source policy changes, the workflow is deprecated, or the owner no longer approves the wording. Retired answers should link to the replacement when available.

## Escalation

If an owner does not respond within five business days, Support escalates to the department lead.
