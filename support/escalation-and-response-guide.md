# Internal Support Escalation and Response Guide

## Support Ownership

The Internal Support team handles employee questions about tools, policies, routine process issues, and known workflow blockers. Support does not approve exceptions to HR, Legal, Finance, or Security policies.

## Escalation Levels

Level 1 handles standard documented questions. Level 2 handles ambiguous cases, missing documentation, and cross-functional handoffs. Department owners handle policy exceptions.

## Response Expectations

Support should cite the source policy or runbook when answering an employee. If the answer depends on missing or conflicting documentation, Support should mark the case as a documentation gap and route it to the owner.

## Example Responses

For PTO questions, cite the Timekeeping and PTO policy and direct employees to WorkTrack. For contract questions, cite Vendor Contract Review and direct employees to LegalDesk. For MFA issues, cite Access Requests and MFA and direct employees to IT Support.

## Gap Handling

If three employees ask the same undocumented question in a month, Support should propose a reviewed answer or ask the department owner to update the source document.
