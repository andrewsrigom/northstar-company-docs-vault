# Billing Questions Triage

Owner: Support and Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-03-12

## Purpose

This page helps Support route billing questions from customers, employees, and internal teams.

## Customer Billing Questions

Support may answer basic invoice status questions using approved account records. Contract disputes, credits, refunds, tax questions, and payment term changes should route to Finance and Legal when needed.

## Internal Billing Questions

Employees asking about vendor invoices should provide vendor name, invoice number, business owner, purchase order if any, and payment urgency.

## Do Not Promise

Support should not promise refunds, credits, payment timing, contract changes, or tax treatment without Finance approval.

## Escalation

Escalate urgent customer billing issues when they block renewal, implementation, or service continuation.
