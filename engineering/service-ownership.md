# Service Ownership

Owner: Engineering
Last reviewed: 2026-03-23

## Purpose

Every production service should have clear ownership so incidents, changes, and questions route quickly.

## Required Fields

Service ownership records should include service name, owning team, technical owner, product owner, support channel, runbook link, repository, deployment path, data classification, and escalation path.

## Owner Responsibilities

Owners maintain runbooks, review alerts, approve material changes, participate in incident response, and coordinate deprecation when a service is retired.

## Ownership Changes

Ownership changes require agreement from current owner, receiving owner, Engineering leadership, and affected Product owner. Update runbooks, dashboards, alerts, and support routing during the transfer.
