# Incident Review Template

Owner: Engineering
Last reviewed: 2026-02-26

## Purpose

Use this template after material production incidents, high-severity customer issues, or repeated operational failures.

## Template

- incident title;
- severity;
- dates and times;
- customer impact;
- internal impact;
- detection method;
- timeline;
- root cause or contributing factors;
- what went well;
- what was hard;
- corrective actions;
- owners and due dates;
- documentation updates needed.

## Review Expectations

Incident reviews should focus on systems and process improvements. Assign follow-ups to owners with due dates and link completed documentation updates.
