# Access Requests and MFA

## Access Request Process

Employees request new system access through AccessDesk. The request must include the business reason, requested role, manager approval, and expected duration when access is temporary.

IT reviews standard access requests within two business days. High-risk systems require approval from the system owner and Security.

## MFA Requirements

MFA is required for email, payroll, CRM, finance tools, source code repositories, and any system that stores customer or employee data.

If an employee loses their MFA device, they should contact IT Support from a known company email address and complete identity verification with their manager copied.

## Offboarding

Managers must submit offboarding requests at least three business days before a planned departure. IT disables access on the employee's final day unless Legal requests a litigation hold or immediate disablement is required.

## Security Notes

Shared accounts are not approved unless Security grants an exception. Exceptions expire after 90 days and must include a named owner.
