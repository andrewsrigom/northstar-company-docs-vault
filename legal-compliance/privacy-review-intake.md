# Privacy Review Intake

Owner: Legal & Compliance
Last reviewed: 2026-03-01

## Purpose

Privacy review helps Northstar understand how customer, prospect, employee, and vendor data is collected, used, shared, stored, and deleted.

## When Review Is Required

Privacy review is required for new vendors that process personal data, new product features that collect personal data, marketing campaigns using prospect lists, analytics changes that track users, and integrations that move data between systems.

## Intake Questions

Requesters should provide:

- business purpose;
- data subjects affected;
- data categories;
- systems involved;
- vendor name if any;
- countries involved;
- retention expectations;
- customer-facing claims or notices.

## Routing

Legal reviews privacy obligations. IT Security reviews technical controls. Product or Operations owns implementation changes depending on the workflow.

## Timing

Submit privacy review at least ten business days before launch or vendor signature. Urgent requests require department leader escalation.
