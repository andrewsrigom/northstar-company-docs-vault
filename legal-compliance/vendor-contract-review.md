# Vendor Contract Review

## When Legal Review Is Required

Legal review is required before signing any contract that includes:

- customer or employee data;
- auto-renewal longer than one year;
- indemnity, limitation of liability, or warranty language;
- spend above 5,000 USD annually;
- regulated data, privacy obligations, or security commitments.

## Review Intake

Submit contract review requests in LegalDesk. Include the draft agreement, business owner, desired signature date, vendor contact, data involved, and purchase request link.

Legal targets initial review within five business days. Urgent reviews require director approval and a clear business deadline.

## Signature Authority

Employees cannot sign vendor contracts unless they are listed in the signature authority matrix. Verbal approval in Slack is not signature authority.

## Record Retention

Executed contracts are stored in ContractSafe. Business owners should not store final signed contracts only in personal drives or email threads.
