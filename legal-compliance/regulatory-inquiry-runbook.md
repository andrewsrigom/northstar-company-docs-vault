# Regulatory Inquiry Runbook

Owner: Legal & Compliance
Last reviewed: 2026-02-08

## Purpose

This runbook explains what employees should do if they receive a government, regulator, law enforcement, or formal legal inquiry.

## Employee Instructions

Do not respond substantively, provide records, delete records, or promise a timeline. Forward the inquiry to Legal immediately and preserve the original message, envelope, attachment, or delivery record.

## Intake Details

Legal needs:

- date received;
- sender identity;
- delivery method;
- deadline stated;
- records requested;
- systems or teams affected;
- whether any customer or employee was named.

## Internal Coordination

Legal coordinates with Security, People Operations, Finance, or Operations depending on the inquiry. External communication must be approved by Legal.
