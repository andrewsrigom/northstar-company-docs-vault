# External Communications Review

Owner: Legal & Compliance
Last reviewed: 2026-03-10

## Purpose

Some external communications require review because they may create legal commitments, disclose non-public information, or affect customers.

## Review Required

Legal or Compliance review is required for:

- customer commitments outside approved contract language;
- public claims about security, privacy, uptime, certifications, or regulatory status;
- statements about roadmap timing or guaranteed functionality;
- press statements about customers, incidents, legal matters, or financial performance;
- testimonials or case studies using customer names.

## Not Usually Required

Routine support replies, approved marketing copy, standard sales emails, and public documentation updates do not require Legal review unless they include unusual commitments or sensitive topics.

## Submission

Submit the draft, target audience, requested publish date, and business owner. Marketing review may also be required for tone, brand, and messaging.
