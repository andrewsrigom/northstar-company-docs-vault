# Internal Glossary

## Purpose

This glossary defines common Northstar terms used across scattered docs, tickets, and meeting notes.

## Terms

`Business owner` means the department leader or delegate accountable for business outcome, budget fit, and adoption.

`System owner` means the team accountable for access, configuration, lifecycle, support routing, and vendor contact.

`Renewal owner` means the person accountable for renewal decision, cancellation timing, contract changes, and budget confirmation.

`Reviewed answer` means a reusable answer approved by the responsible policy owner after Support or a department documents a repeated question.

`Documentation gap` means a missing, stale, conflicting, or ambiguous source that prevents a confident answer.

`Launch gate` means a checklist item that must be complete before customer-facing release or external announcement.

`Exception` means an approved deviation from a standard policy. Exceptions need an owner, expiration date, and written rationale.

## Common Confusion

A requester is not always the business owner. A tool admin is not always the renewal owner. A manager can approve workflow timing but may not approve spend, legal terms, or security exceptions.
