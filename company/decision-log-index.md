# Decision Log Index

## Purpose

Northstar teams keep decision notes in several places. This index explains what must be captured when a decision changes policy, process, customer commitments, or cross-functional ownership.

## Required Decision Fields

Every durable decision note should include:

- decision date;
- decision owner;
- teams consulted;
- options considered;
- selected option;
- expected impact;
- follow-up owner;
- review date or expiration date.

## Where Decisions Live

- Product decisions live in roadmap or launch folders.
- Engineering architecture decisions live in engineering design docs.
- People policy decisions live in People & HR policy pages.
- Vendor and procurement decisions live in Operations vendor folders.
- Contract and privacy decisions live in Legal & Compliance records.

## Promotion Rule

If a decision is referenced more than three times in a month, the owner should promote it into a reviewed page or update an existing policy. Slack threads and meeting notes should not be the only long-term source.
