# Operating Cadence

## Purpose

This page describes the recurring planning and review rhythm used by Northstar teams. Individual departments may keep more detailed calendars.

## Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: team planning and blockers.
- Tuesday through Thursday: customer delivery, product development, and cross-functional reviews.
- Friday: async status updates, Finance approval cleanup, incident review follow-ups, and documentation maintenance.

## Monthly Rhythm

Department leads submit a monthly business review note by the fifth business day. The note should include progress against goals, material risks, customer or employee impacts, and decisions needed from leadership.

Finance closes prior-month spend by the eighth business day. Department owners should resolve missing invoices, purchase orders, and budget questions before close.

## Quarterly Rhythm

Quarterly planning starts six weeks before the next quarter. Product drafts roadmap themes, Sales and Customer Success submit customer signal summaries, Engineering provides capacity guidance, and Finance confirms target budget envelopes.

## Documentation Expectation

Every recurring forum should have an owner, agenda, decision log, and location for artifacts. If the same question appears in two consecutive meetings, the forum owner should create or update a durable documentation page.
