# Documentation Standards

## Purpose

This standard helps teams write internal pages that employees can actually use in search, chat, and ticket triage.

## Minimum Page Structure

A durable policy or runbook should include:

- purpose;
- owner;
- when to use it;
- steps or rules;
- escalation path;
- last reviewed date.

## Naming

Use plain titles that match employee language. Prefer `Expense Reimbursements` over `Spend Operations Workflow v3`. Add synonyms in the body when employees use multiple names for the same process.

## Stale Content

Pages older than twelve months should be reviewed before they are used for high-impact answers involving payroll, legal obligations, security, customer commitments, or spend approval.

## Archived Pages

Archived pages must start with `Status: archived` and link to the current replacement when one exists. Archived pages should remain searchable only when they explain historical decisions.
