# Mutual Action Plan Template

Owner: Sales
Last reviewed: 2026-01-26

## Purpose

A mutual action plan helps Sales and prospects coordinate evaluation, procurement, legal review, and launch milestones.

## Sections

- business outcome;
- evaluation criteria;
- stakeholders;
- security review;
- legal review;
- procurement steps;
- target decision date;
- implementation owner;
- risks and blockers;
- next meeting.

## Internal Notes

Keep internal negotiation strategy out of customer-visible versions. Customer-facing plans should not include unapproved discounts, roadmap commitments, or legal assumptions.
