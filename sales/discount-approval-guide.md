# Discount Approval Guide

Owner: Sales Operations and Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-02-09

## Purpose

This guide explains approval expectations for non-standard pricing and discounts.

## Standard Discounts

Account executives may request standard discounts within approved ranges in the quoting system. Manager approval is required before sending the quote.

## Additional Review

Finance review is required for material discounts, unusual payment terms, multi-year structures, credits, or customer-specific commercial exceptions. Legal review is required when pricing terms change contract language.

## Documentation

Discount requests should include customer context, business case, competitor pressure if relevant, requested terms, approval deadline, and renewal impact.

## Do Not Promise

Sales should not promise discounts, credits, payment terms, or renewal protections before approvals are complete.
