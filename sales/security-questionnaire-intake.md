# Security Questionnaire Intake

Owner: Sales Operations and IT Security
Last reviewed: 2026-03-02

## Purpose

Customers may request security questionnaires during sales or renewal cycles. This process routes those requests to the right owner.

## Intake Requirements

Submit customer name, opportunity or renewal link, due date, questionnaire format, requested evidence, security topics, customer contact, and whether a contract deadline depends on completion.

## Routing

Sales Operations checks completeness. IT Security owns technical security answers. Legal reviews privacy, regulatory, contractual, and legal representation questions.

## Reuse

Use approved answer library content where possible. Do not invent security claims or modify certification language without IT Security approval.

## Timing

Standard questionnaires need five business days. Complex questionnaires or portal-based submissions may need ten business days.
