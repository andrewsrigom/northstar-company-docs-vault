# Lead Routing And Handoff

Owner: Sales Operations
Last reviewed: 2026-03-04

## Purpose

This process explains how inbound leads, partner referrals, and campaign responses are routed.

## Routing Inputs

Routing uses region, company size, account ownership, industry, source campaign, and existing customer status.

## Handoff Requirements

Marketing-qualified leads should include source, form details, campaign, consent status, company domain, requested follow-up, and any declared timeline.

## Existing Customers

Existing customer inquiries should route to Customer Success unless the request is clearly a new commercial opportunity requiring Sales involvement.

## Data Issues

Sales Operations reviews duplicate accounts, missing domains, bounced emails, and suspicious submissions.
