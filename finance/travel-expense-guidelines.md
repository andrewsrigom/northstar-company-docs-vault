# Travel Expense Guidelines

Owner: Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-02-05

## Purpose

This page explains standard business travel expense expectations for Northstar employees.

## Approval Before Travel

Business travel must be approved by the employee's manager before booking. Travel expected to exceed 2,000 USD or involve customer entertainment requires department lead approval. International travel also requires People Operations review when work location rules may apply.

## Airfare and Lodging

Employees should book economy airfare unless an exception is approved for medical accommodation, unusually long travel, or customer contract requirements. Lodging should be reasonable for the city and event.

## Meals

Meals are reimbursable when travel requires an overnight stay or a business meeting during normal meal hours. Receipts are required for meals over 25 USD. Alcohol is not reimbursable unless a department leader approves a customer event in advance.

## Submission

Submit travel expenses in SpendFlow within 30 days after the trip ends. Attach receipts, business purpose, attendee list for customer meals, and approved travel request when available.
