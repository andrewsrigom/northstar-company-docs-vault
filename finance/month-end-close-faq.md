# Month-End Close FAQ

Owner: Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-03-18

## Purpose

This FAQ explains common questions during Finance month-end close.

## What Employees Need To Do

Employees should submit expenses, missing receipts, invoice owner confirmations, and purchase card coding by the third business day of the month.

## What Managers Need To Do

Managers should approve reimbursements, review outstanding invoices, and confirm unclear business owners before the fifth business day.

## Why Close Matters

Late approvals can affect budget reporting, vendor payment timing, and department forecasts. Finance may accrue expected expenses when invoices are missing but the service period is known.

## Common Issues

- invoice lacks a purchase order;
- vendor sent invoice to an employee instead of AP;
- requester is no longer at the company;
- expense category is unclear;
- spend belongs to a different department.
