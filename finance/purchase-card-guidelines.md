# Purchase Card Guidelines

Owner: Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-03-11

## Purpose

Purchase cards are for approved business expenses that cannot reasonably go through invoice or purchase order flow.

## Allowed Uses

- approved software trials under 500 USD;
- team supplies with manager approval;
- event materials with Marketing or Operations approval;
- emergency travel changes;
- small vendor charges where invoicing is unavailable.

## Prohibited Uses

Do not use purchase cards for personal expenses, payroll items, gift cards, recurring software without system owner approval, contract commitments, or purchases that bypass required Legal or IT Security review.

## Receipt Rules

Cardholders must upload receipts and business purpose in SpendFlow within five business days. Missing receipts may result in card suspension.

## Lost or Compromised Cards

Report lost, stolen, or suspicious card activity to Finance immediately. Finance coordinates replacement and disputed charges.
