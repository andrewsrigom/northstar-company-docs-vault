# Reimbursements and Invoice Intake

## Expense Reimbursements

Employees submit reimbursable expenses in SpendFlow within 30 days of the transaction. Receipts are required for expenses over 25 USD.

Manager approval is required for all reimbursements. Finance audits meals, travel, subscriptions, and hardware purchases monthly.

## Invoice Intake

Vendors should send invoices to ap@northstar.example. The invoice must include vendor legal name, invoice number, purchase order when available, amount, tax details, and payment instructions.

Finance targets invoice review within five business days. Missing purchase orders or unclear business owners may delay payment.

## Budget Changes

Department budget changes require a written explanation from the department lead and Finance review. Changes above 10,000 USD require executive approval.

## Common Rejections

Finance rejects reimbursements when receipts are missing, the expense is submitted after 30 days without explanation, or the expense was not business-related.
