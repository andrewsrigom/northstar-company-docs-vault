# Budget Planning Calendar

Owner: Finance
Last reviewed: 2026-04-01

## Purpose

This calendar gives department owners a simple view of the annual budget planning process.

## Annual Planning

- August: Finance shares planning assumptions and prior-year spend exports.
- September: department owners submit headcount, vendor, travel, and program requests.
- October: Finance reviews scenarios with department leads.
- November: executive review and tradeoff decisions.
- December: final budget communication and system setup.

## Monthly Forecast Updates

Department owners should flag forecast changes above 10,000 USD, unexpected renewals, delayed hiring, or customer-driven program changes. Finance reviews changes during monthly business review.

## Vendor Renewals

Renewals above 5,000 USD should be reviewed at least 60 days before renewal date. Operations owns the renewal calendar, but department owners own business need and budget fit.
