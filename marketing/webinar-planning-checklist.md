# Webinar Planning Checklist

Owner: Marketing
Last reviewed: 2026-02-24

## Purpose

This checklist helps teams plan webinars with customers, partners, or internal subject matter experts.

## Planning

- define audience and registration goal;
- confirm speaker availability;
- draft title and abstract;
- prepare landing page;
- create promotional copy;
- confirm recording plan;
- create sales follow-up notes;
- prepare support FAQ if customer-facing.

## Review Requirements

Legal review is required for customer speakers, partner commitments, data claims, and recording consent language. Product review is required for roadmap or demo content.

## After The Event

Marketing uploads recording, shares attendee list with Sales when permitted, publishes follow-up assets, and reports registration, attendance, and pipeline influence.
