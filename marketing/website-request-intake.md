# Website Request Intake

Owner: Marketing
Last reviewed: 2026-03-17

## Purpose

This page explains how teams request website updates, landing pages, copy edits, tracking changes, or campaign pages.

## Request Fields

Website requests should include page URL, requested change, business reason, target date, audience, copy owner, design needs, analytics requirements, and review approvals.

## Priority

Marketing prioritizes legal or security corrections, launch blockers, broken conversion paths, paid campaign pages, customer-facing errors, and executive-approved initiatives.

## Review

Product reviews feature descriptions. Legal reviews regulated claims, customer references, privacy language, and unusual commitments. Data Analytics reviews tracking or dashboard changes.

## Service Target

Small copy changes are usually reviewed within three business days. New landing pages should be requested at least fifteen business days before launch.
