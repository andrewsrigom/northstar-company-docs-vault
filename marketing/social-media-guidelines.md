# Social Media Guidelines

Owner: Marketing
Last reviewed: 2026-01-19

## Purpose

These guidelines explain how employees can talk about Northstar on public social channels.

## Employees May Share

Employees may share published company posts, public job openings, approved event pages, public documentation, and their own general professional commentary.

## Do Not Share

Do not share non-public roadmap, customer names without approval, screenshots of internal tools, private Slack conversations, security findings, financial results, or unannounced partnerships.

## Personal Accounts

Employees are not required to post company content. When sharing personal opinions related to work, avoid implying that the post is an official company statement.

## Escalation

Route media inquiries, incident questions, legal threats, or posts about customers to Marketing and Legal before responding.
