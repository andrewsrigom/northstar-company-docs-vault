# Customer Story Approval

Owner: Marketing
Last reviewed: 2026-02-16

## Purpose

Customer stories require coordinated approval because they use customer names, outcomes, quotes, or metrics.

## Intake

Marketing needs the customer contact, account owner, proposed story angle, proof points, quotes, usage details, and publication channel.

## Approval Path

Customer Success confirms customer relationship context. Sales confirms commercial sensitivity. Legal reviews consent language and usage rights. Marketing owns final copy and publication.

## Metrics and Claims

Do not publish customer metrics unless the customer approves the exact numbers and Legal approves the claim. If the customer approves a vague outcome, keep the language qualitative.

## Storage

Final approvals, signed releases, and approved copy should be stored in the campaign folder. Slack approval alone is not enough for publication.
