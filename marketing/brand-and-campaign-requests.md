# Brand and Campaign Requests

## Brand Review

Marketing reviews customer-facing copy, public launch pages, paid campaigns, event materials, and partner announcements. Internal-only team messages do not require brand review unless they include external commitments.

## Campaign Intake

Campaign requests should be submitted in MarketDesk at least ten business days before the desired launch date. The request should include audience, objective, offer, channels, compliance considerations, and approval deadline.

## Legal and Compliance Routing

Marketing routes campaigns to Legal when claims mention pricing, guarantees, regulated industries, customer data, or partner commitments.

## Approved Assets

Approved logos, colors, boilerplate, and slide templates live in the Brand Kit folder. Teams should not reuse old decks as the source of truth for brand assets.
