# Campaign Launch Brief Template

Owner: Marketing
Last reviewed: 2026-03-06

## Purpose

Use this template for campaigns that involve public messaging, customer announcements, partner activity, paid media, webinars, or coordinated sales outreach.

## Required Fields

- campaign name;
- business goal;
- target audience;
- primary message;
- launch date;
- channels;
- offer or call to action;
- dependencies;
- review owners;
- success metrics.

## Required Reviews

Legal review is required for customer names, security claims, regulated claims, pricing claims, or competitive comparisons. Product review is required when a campaign mentions roadmap, beta features, or release timing.

## Launch Readiness

Marketing should confirm approved copy, landing page, tracking links, sales enablement, support notes, and customer-facing FAQ before launch.
