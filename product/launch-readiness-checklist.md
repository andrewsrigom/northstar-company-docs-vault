# Product Launch Readiness Checklist

Owner: Product
Last reviewed: 2026-03-25

## Purpose

This checklist helps teams prepare for customer-facing product launches.

## Readiness Items

- Product owner assigned;
- Engineering owner assigned;
- release plan confirmed;
- rollback or mitigation plan documented;
- Support notes prepared;
- Customer Success enablement prepared;
- Sales enablement prepared when needed;
- Marketing launch brief completed;
- Legal review completed for claims or commitments;
- success metrics defined.

## Launch Gate

Product can delay launch when support readiness, legal approval, security review, or release rollback plan is incomplete.
