# Product Discovery Intake

Owner: Product
Last reviewed: 2026-03-07

## Purpose

This intake helps Product evaluate new ideas, customer requests, and internal workflow problems.

## Required Context

Requesters should provide problem statement, user type, current workaround, evidence, frequency, business impact, affected accounts, and urgency.

## Not Discovery Ready

Ideas are not ready for discovery when they only describe a solution, lack a user problem, or cannot identify who is affected.

## Product Review

Product reviews intake weekly and may route items to discovery, backlog, research, support documentation, or no action.

## Communication

Product should communicate whether an item was accepted for discovery. Acceptance for discovery is not a roadmap commitment.
