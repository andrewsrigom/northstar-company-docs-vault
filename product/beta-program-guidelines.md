# Beta Program Guidelines

Owner: Product
Last reviewed: 2026-02-22

## Purpose

Beta programs help Product learn from limited customer usage before broad release.

## Eligibility

Beta customers should have a clear use case, engaged owner, agreed feedback cadence, and no blocking contractual restriction. Customer Success confirms relationship fit.

## Requirements

Each beta should have goals, entry criteria, exit criteria, support path, risk notes, feedback plan, and customer communication.

## Legal Review

Legal review is required when beta terms differ from standard contract language, involve sensitive data, or create unusual support or uptime expectations.

## Closing The Beta

Product should document what was learned, what changed, and whether the feature moves to launch, more discovery, or cancellation.
