# Roadmap Planning

Owner: Product
Last reviewed: 2026-04-03

## Purpose

Roadmap planning aligns customer problems, business goals, engineering capacity, and go-to-market timing.

## Inputs

- customer feedback;
- support trends;
- sales opportunities;
- renewal risks;
- product analytics;
- engineering reliability work;
- security and compliance needs;
- company goals.

## Planning Cycle

Product drafts themes six weeks before the quarter. Engineering estimates capacity and risk. Customer Success and Sales validate customer impact. Leadership confirms priorities during quarterly planning.

## Roadmap Communication

External roadmap communication requires Product and Legal approval. Sales should not promise delivery dates unless the commitment is in an approved customer agreement.
