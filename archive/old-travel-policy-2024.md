## Old Guidance

The previous guidance allowed manager-approved travel without department lead review for most trips. It did not include the current 2,000 USD escalation threshold and did not address international remote work review.

## Why It Changed

Finance updated travel guidance after repeated confusion about customer meals, international work locations, and late expense submissions.
