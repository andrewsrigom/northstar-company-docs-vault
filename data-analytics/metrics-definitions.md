# Metrics Definitions

Owner: Data Analytics
Last reviewed: 2026-03-19

## Purpose

This page defines common operating metrics so teams use consistent language.

## Definitions

`Active customer` means a contracted customer with active service during the reporting period.

`Activated account` means a customer that completed the onboarding milestone defined by Customer Success.

`Qualified pipeline` means opportunities accepted by Sales under current qualification rules.

`Gross renewal risk` means renewal value associated with accounts flagged high risk before mitigation.

`Support first response time` means elapsed business time from case creation to first human response.

## Metric Changes

Metric definition changes require Data Analytics review and communication to affected dashboard owners.
