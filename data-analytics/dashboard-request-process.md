# Dashboard Request Process

Owner: Data Analytics
Last reviewed: 2026-02-06

## Purpose

This process helps teams request useful dashboards instead of one-off charts that become stale.

## Request Fields

Requesters should provide decision to support, audience, metrics needed, filters, refresh frequency, source systems, deadline, and whether numbers will be shared externally.

## Prioritization

Data Analytics prioritizes executive reporting, customer commitments, compliance needs, repeated manual reporting, and launch measurement.

## External Use

Metrics intended for customers, prospects, investors, or public use require owner review and may require Legal or Marketing review.

## Maintenance

Every dashboard should have a business owner and review date. Dashboards without usage for 90 days may be archived.
